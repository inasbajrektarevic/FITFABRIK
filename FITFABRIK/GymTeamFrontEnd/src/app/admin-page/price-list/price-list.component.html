BMI Advice for Our Customers

Personalized BMI Advice

Underweight (BMI < 18.5)

It's important to consult with a healthcare provider to determine any underlying causes of being underweight. Consider a balanced diet rich in calories, focusing on whole grains, protein-rich foods, and healthy fats. Regular strength training exercises can help build muscle mass.

Normal weight (BMI 18.5 - 24.9)

You’re in a healthy weight range. Maintain your weight by eating a balanced diet and staying physically active. Regular cardio and strength training are recommended to keep your body strong and healthy.

Overweight (BMI 25 - 29.9)

Consider adopting a healthier diet that focuses on nutrient-dense foods, portion control, and regular exercise. Incorporating both cardio and strength training into your routine will help you manage your weight effectively.

Obese (BMI 30 or higher)

It’s important to work closely with a healthcare provider to create a safe and effective weight loss plan. A combination of diet changes, increased physical activity, and behavioral changes is often recommended. Focus on reducing calorie intake while increasing daily physical activity.
